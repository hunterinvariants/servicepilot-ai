ABOUT SERVICEPILOT AI

Built for the work between a request and a resolution.

ServicePilot AI is an open-source portfolio application demonstrating how AI can participate in real service operations without removing human accountability.

Why it exists

Most service teams receive incomplete requests across forms, email, chat and APIs. ServicePilot structures that information, detects uncertainty and risk, and prepares a service plan an operator can review.

What makes it different

The product treats AI output as a recommendation, not authority. Appointments, quotes and external integrations remain behind an explicit approval boundary, while each decision becomes part of an auditable history.

Built in the open

The complete FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Redis, Docker and CI implementation is available on GitHub for inspection and extension.
