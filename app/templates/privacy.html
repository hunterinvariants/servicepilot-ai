PRIVACY

Clear data practices.

This hosted instance is a portfolio demonstration. Submit only fictional or non-sensitive service-request information.

Data collected

Service requests may include a name, email address, telephone number, service address, message and requested appointment window. Operational users also create approval and audit records.

Processing and storage

Data is stored in the instance's PostgreSQL database. The hosted demo uses a deterministic local classifier; submitted request content is not sent to OpenAI or another external AI provider. HTTPS is provided through Cloudflare.

Retention and requests

This demonstration does not promise a specific retention period and is not intended for production personal data. Contact the project owner through the project repository for deletion or access requests.

Production responsibility

Organizations deploying ServicePilot are responsible for selecting appropriate hosting, retention, subprocessors, lawful bases and data-processing agreements for their jurisdiction.
